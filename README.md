# AI Video Detection: Generalizing to SOTA Generators

> **"Space lies, Time tells the truth."** > While modern generators (Sora, PyramidFlow) have nearly closed the spectral gap in individual frames, temporal coherence remains the definitive "fingerprint" for detection.

This repository presents a systematic study on detecting artificially generated videos using **3D Vision Transformer (3D-ViT)** architectures. The project explores how to bridge the "generalization gap" toward State-of-the-Art (SOTA) generators through forensic augmentation techniques and **multi-scale temporal sampling**.

---

## 🖼️ Overview: Is this REAL or AI?

![AI Generated Fireworks](/results/fireworks.gif)

*At first glance, modern SOTA generators produce photorealistic textures that can easily deceive the human eye. **Can you spot the AI?*** 

As generators close the spectral gap in spatial details, visual inspection is no longer enough. Our research focuses on bridging the **Generalization Gap**: since modern fakes exhibit nearly perfect spatial artifacts, we leverage **temporal inconsistencies** and **multi-scale dynamics** to distinguish real from synthetic content.

## 🛠️ Methodology & Innovation

### 1. Multi-Scale 3D-ViT Architecture
Instead of relying on a fixed frame rate, the model samples clips at 4, 8, and 16 frames. This approach captures both high-frequency *flickering* and long-term dynamic inconsistencies.

![Architecture Diagram - Tubelet Embedding and 3D-ViT Flow](/results/temporal_analysis.png)

### 2. Wavelet Blending & Frequency Training
To force the model to learn semantic and temporal features (rather than simple compression shortcuts), we implemented **Wavelet Blending**:
* **Decomposition (DWT):** Splits frames into approximation and detail components.
* **The Swap:** Replaces high-frequency detail components (CD) of a fake frame with those from a real video.
* **Goal:** Creates a "Clean Fake" that challenges the classifier to find anomalies in motion and content rather than frequency fingerprints.

![Wavelet Diagram - Creation of 'Clean Fake'](/results/clean_fake.png)

### 3. Explainability: Why are SOTA fakes "hard"?
Using **Stationary Wavelet Transform (SWT)** analysis, we discovered that the most challenging videos show an energy profile in the mid-frequency bands that is almost identical to real videos.

![SWT Energy Analysis Graph](/results/swt_analysis_simple_cD.png)

## ⚠️ Limitations & Conclusions
Despite reaching **97.53% accuracy**, the study highlights a "Confidence Collapse" on the most sophisticated samples. The battle between generators and detectors is an "arms race" where temporal robustness currently serves as our strongest defensive edge.
